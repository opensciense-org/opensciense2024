ROSE Festival

@CBSoft 2024

For the first time, there will be a ROSE Festival at CBSoft! The goal is to create a venue where researchers can share and learn practices on artifact sharing, organization, documentation, etc., and receive public credit for facilitating and participating in Open Science in Software Engineering.

Authors of artifacts successfully evaluated in the OpenScienSE Artifact Evaluation Track will be invited to lightning talks about their artifacts.
